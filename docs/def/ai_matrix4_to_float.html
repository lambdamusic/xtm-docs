ai_matrix4_to_float   xtlang

Defined in:  https://github.com/digego/extempore/tree/v0.8.9/libs/external/assimp.xtm

(bind-func ai_matrix4_to_float
  (lambda (mat1:aiMatrix4x4* mat2:float*)
    (doloop (i 16)
      (pset! mat2 i (aref (convert mat1 |16,float|*) i)))))
